About Us

Our Parish History

St. Veronica Catholic Church was established in 1985 to serve the growing Catholic community in Karmem III, Makurdi. Named after St. Veronica who showed compassion to Jesus on His way to Calvary, our parish continues this tradition of service and compassion to all.

Over the years, we have grown from a small mission church to a vibrant parish with over 2,000 registered families. Our church building was completed in 2001 and has since been a beacon of faith in our community.
